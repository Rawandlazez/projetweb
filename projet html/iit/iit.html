INTERNATIONAL INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY (IIT)

It is a private higher education organization approved by the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research. It was inaugurated on August 23, 2012.

Why We Choise IIT? we choose it because it:
  • Propose an innovative education model in partnership with universities and schools at the national and international level, by setting up a semester-based organization and programs inspired by the North American model.
  • Develop new skills necessary for future Tunisian and international students, particularly in the areas of analysis, problem solving, critical thinking and innovation.
  • Respond to the needs of regional and national businesses, by building a network of local and international industrial partners and experts.
Goals and Objectives of the institue:
  • The establishment of the IIT was with the objective of offering students a favorable environment and practical training based on an integrated educational system fully in line with professional needs and the labor market in Tunisia and abroad.

  • Approved training courses that comply with national and international standards.

  • The degrees, designed with some national and international experts, are established in accordance with national curricula and also international academic standards. Our teaching staff, our trainers and our experts are in direct contact with educational, scientific and professional news. All this in order to provide students with practical training and enable them to respond positively to the needs of professionals and recruiters.

    • Continuing education and doctoral exchanges with Western universities.
  • Our exchange programs and our partnerships give our students the opportunity to continue their studies in several Canadian, American and French university cycles.

  • Continuing education allows our teaching staff and our graduate students to get up to speed while continuing a professional activity. Competitiveness and excellence of know-how are the desired goals.

VALUES AND PROMISES
    The core values ​​that led to the creation of the International Institute of Technology are:

  • Cooperation and complementarity:
    • The ITI has established cooperative and complementary relationships with several regional and international academic institutions:
    Like the Faculty of Sciences of SFAX (FSS), the Preparatory Institute for Engineering Studies of SFAX (IPEIS), the National School of Electronics and Telecommunications (ENET'Com), the Higher Institute of Industrial Management of SFAX (ISGI), the Higher Institute of Computing and Multimedia of SFAX (ISIMS), the Higher Institute of Technological Studies of SFAX (ISET).
    As for international institutions, it quote:
    University of Engineering of Sherbrooke, School of Computer Engineering of France (EPSI), Globe University (United States), University of Quebec at Trois Rivières and that of Moncton (Canada) and Canadian University of Dubai (Dubai).


  • Research, innovation and creativity:
    • The IIT pays particular attention to scientific research, particularly in the field of renewable energies, the environment, computer security, 3D printing, information and communication (ICT). It has created a research, development and creativity center, as well as a manufacturing laboratory (FABLAB), The Institute also has an incubator that facilitates and encourages young graduates to launch their start-ups in a competitive environment.

  • Continuing education:
    • In order to further strengthen ties with companies, the IIT created in December 2013 a training center called “IIT Training”. Its main vocation is to provide business executives as well as students and graduates with opportunities to pursue continuing education in specialized fields related to languages, computer science and others. These certifying and à la carte training courses offer the target audience opportunities to advance in their careers.

  • Competitiveness:
    • The ITI attaches great importance to the quality of the teachers recruited, their skills, their capacity for continuous improvement and their application of active pedagogy and new teaching techniques.

  • Respect and well-being:
    • Our institution is committed to providing its students and researchers with a supportive environment that is both modern and well suited to ensure the highest quality of university life.
